The Logo

This logo was created by Mr. Nic Oswald Borines, CAA faculty. The main shape of the logo is hexagonal to represent the six (6) major discipline in Agriculture namely; Crop Science, Soil Science, Crop Protection, Animal Science, Agricultural Economics and Agricultural Extension.

Color Composition

Yellow - represents light and direction; the color of energy.
Green - represents life and vigor; the color and the main goal of agriculture.
Gold - represents abundance and wealth; the color of prosperity.
Blue - represents depth and intelligence; the color of wisdom.
Brown - represents stability and reliability; the color of earth.

Symbolisms

1) Diamond (top) – represents the Sun which illuminates all beings and is the ultimate source of all energy.
2) Crops and Animals (upper left) – represents the main subjects of Agriculture.
3) Gear, Pipe, and Products in baskets (upper right) – represents Industry in Agriculture; derived from the name of the college.
4) Land (bottom) – represents the earth; the foundation of Agriculture and Agri-Industries.
5) Flask (center) – general representation of Science in the field of Agriculture
